AVERKORF DUNGEON
----------------
This is meant to be a small roguelike game made as a side project. 
In this game you will be using the arrow keys in order to select a wanted command and then carrying it out by pressing enter.
You are taking the place of an adventurer, who wants to obtain some treasure. The goal is to navigate enough rooms to get to the treasure and then get out.
The game is meant to be completed in one sitting.
